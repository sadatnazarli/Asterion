## The Asterion Edge
**Mission:** Find misunderstood change before it becomes obvious, while controlling downside risk.

Asterion is a probabilistic investment intelligence system. It does not predict the future; it maps the present better than consensus. By leveraging deterministic SEC facts and unstructured RAG evidence, Asterion continuously calculates the mathematical gap between *ground truth reality* and *market-implied expectations*. 

We win by detecting state transitions—when a company's fundamental reality shifts, but the market's narrative has not yet updated.
