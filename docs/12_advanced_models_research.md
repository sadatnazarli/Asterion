## Asterion Advanced Models Research Report
**Date:** Current
**Status:** Approved for M5 integration

### Core Philosophy
Models must capture causal market mechanisms (expectation gaps, convexity, reflexivity). We reject "black box" price prediction in favor of probabilistic state estimation and reality-to-expectations gap analysis.

### Approved P0/P1 Models
1. **Reverse DCF (Expectations Gap):** Solves for market-implied growth.
2. **Bayesian Thesis Updating:** Formalizes RAG evidence into probability shifts.
3. **KL Divergence (Narrative Entropy):** Measures narrative focus vs fragmentation.
4. **Operating Leverage Convexity:** Deterministic M2 ratio analysis.
5. **SBC Reflexivity Risk:** Deterministic cash flow vulnerability.

### Rejected Models
- Neural Networks for price prediction (Non-stationary overfitting).
- Fourier Analysis (Markets are not periodic).
- Black-Litterman (Too fragile for MVP).
